About This e-Portfolio

This e-Portfolio was developed as part of the Intelligent Agents module within the MSc Artificial Intelligence programme at the University of Essex Online. It presents evidence of my academic learning, analytical skills, and professional development across the duration of the module.

About Me

My name is Naeema Alnaqbi. I am a biomedical engineer working within healthcare and pre-hospital operational environments, alongside postgraduate study in artificial intelligence. My professional interests lie at the intersection of intelligent systems, decision support, healthcare technology, and responsible AI deployment.

Through my academic work, I focus on how intelligent agents and autonomous systems can support complex, safety-critical environments while maintaining accountability, transparency, and ethical integrity.

Purpose of the e-Portfolio

The purpose of this e-Portfolio is to demonstrate achievement of the module learning outcomes by presenting:

  • Critical engagement with intelligent agent theory and architectures
  • Applied analysis through collaborative discussions and activities
  • Evidence of professional and ethical reasoning
  • Reflective evaluation of learning and skill development

Academic Integrity and AI Use

All content presented in this e-Portfolio is my own work. Artificial intelligence tools were used only for limited support purposes such as language checking and formatting, in accordance with University of Essex academic integrity guidelines.

No content was generated automatically without critical review, and all academic sources are appropriately cited using the required referencing style.

Programme Context

This e-Portfolio contributes to the formal assessment for the Intelligent Agents module and is submitted as part of the MSc Artificial Intelligence degree. It reflects not only technical knowledge, but also transferable skills including critical thinking, collaboration, and ethical awareness.
