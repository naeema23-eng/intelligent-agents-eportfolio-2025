About This e-Portfolio

This e-Portfolio was created as part of the Intelligent Agents module of the MSc Artificial Intelligence programme at the University of Essex Online. It documents my progress, reflections, and assessed work throughout Units 1–12.

About Me

My name is Naeema Abdalla Ahmed Alnaqbi. I am a biomedical engineer working in prehospital care and medical equipment management, and a postgraduate AI student. My academic and professional interests include intelligent systems, machine learning, healthcare technology, and AI applications in emergency response.

Purpose
