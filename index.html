Module e-Portfolio Overview

This e-Portfolio collects my key activities, discussions and reflections for the Intelligent Agents module (Oct 2025 cohort) as part of the MSc Artificial Intelligence at the University of Essex Online.

The final assessed e-Portfolio is worth 40% of the module mark with a total word limit of 2,500 words:

  • Up to 1,500 words across the e-Portfolio activities (discussions & exercises)
  • A 1,000-word final reflection on my learning and contribution

Screenshots of my posts, diagrams and code examples will be included where relevant and do not count towards the word limit.

How This Links to the Final Reflection

Throughout the module I will use the “What? – So What? – Now What?” reflective model to connect each activity to my learning:

  • What? – Brief description of the activity or incident
  • So What? – Analysis, feelings, what I learned
  • Now What? – How I will apply this learning in future

By the end of Unit 12, this will support a coherent final reflection that demonstrates my understanding of agent-based computing, communication languages, deep learning, and their application to real-world problems in my professional context.
