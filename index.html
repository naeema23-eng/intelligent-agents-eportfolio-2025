Welcome to my e-Portfolio

This e-portfolio presents my work for the module Research Methods and Professional Practice on the MSc Artificial Intelligence at the University of Essex Online. It combines collaborative discussions, proposal planning, quantitative skills tasks, and reflection, grounded in my professional context as a biomedical engineer interested in AI for healthcare.
