Welcome to my e-Portfolio

This e-portfolio presents my work for the module Intelligent Agents on the MSc Artificial Intelligence at the University of Essex Online. It combines collaborative discussions, learning evidence, and reflective writing, with a focus on intelligent and multi-agent systems in modern professional contexts.

Module: Intelligent Agents Academic year: 2025–2026 Student: Naeema Alnaqbi

Portfolio structure

Pages
  • Discussions: Discussion 1–3 (initial posts, peer responses, and summary posts) in one page.
  • Research & Proposal: Research awareness and key literature engagement supporting module work.
  • Statistics & Data: Evaluation evidence, figures, and interpretations.
  • Reflections: Reflective writing aligned to learning outcomes.
  • Activities: Practical tasks and supporting evidence.
