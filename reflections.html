Final Reflection (1,000 Words)

This page will contain my final reflective narrative for the Intelligent Agents module. The reflection will follow the Rolfe et al. (2001) model:

  • What? – Description of key activities, discussions, group work, and learning moments.
  • So What? – Emotional response, analysis, and interpretation. What did I learn?
  • Now What? – Application of learning, future actions, and how this shapes my development.

The reflection will integrate evidence from:

  • Collaborative Discussion 1, 2, and 3
  • Seminar preparation tasks
  • Unit 6 group dialogue activity
  • Parse Tree activity (Unit 8)
  • Deep Learning in Action (Unit 10)

This section will be written and finalised at the end of Unit 12.
